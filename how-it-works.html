How PayslipMax Works

A simple and secure process to manage all your payslips in one place

1

Download the App

Start by downloading the PayslipMax app from the App Store or Google Play Store. The app is free and available for both iOS and Android devices.

2

Upload Your Payslips

Upload your payslips directly from the app, or use our website to upload them from your computer. You can drag and drop PDF files or select them from your device.

For sensitive documents, you can add password protection to ensure your data remains secure.

3

Automatic Data Extraction

PayslipMax uses advanced technology to automatically extract key information from your payslips, including:

  • Gross pay
  • Net pay
  • Tax deductions
  • Benefits and allowances
  • Company information

All processing happens on your device, so your sensitive data never leaves your control.

4

Manage and Analyze

Once your payslips are processed, you can:

  • View a summary of all your income and deductions
  • Track changes in your salary over time
  • Compare different pay periods
  • Search for specific information
  • Export summaries for tax preparation
5

Secure Access Anytime

Access your payslip data anytime, anywhere, even when offline. Your data is securely stored on your device and protected with industry-standard encryption.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. PayslipMax uses end-to-end encryption and processes all data locally on your device. Your payslips are never sent to external servers for processing, ensuring maximum privacy.

What file formats are supported?

Currently, PayslipMax supports PDF payslips. We're working on adding support for image formats and direct connections to popular payroll systems.

How accurate is the data extraction?

Our algorithm accurately extracts data from most standard payslip formats. For unusual or complex formats, you can manually verify and edit the extracted information.

Is there a limit to how many payslips I can upload?

The free version allows up to 10 payslips. Premium subscribers enjoy unlimited uploads and additional features like advanced analytics and export options.
